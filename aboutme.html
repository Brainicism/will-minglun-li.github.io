About Me!



Welcome to my About Me page!

My legal name is Ming Lun Li, but I prefer to be called Will as that's what people call me/know me as after I came to Canada. I'm also a self-motivated and dedicated software engineer/programmer.

I study Computer Engineering (Honours, Co-op) at the University of Waterloo and I am always excited to learn about new technology and solving fun/challenging problems. I am very interested in web development, game making/development, and a variety of other software engineering/development fields.

I have always aspired to become a programmer who can eventually earn a living developing software that people use and love. I first started programming in my grade 10 computer science course where I learned and gained an extensive amount of knowledge in C++. I later went on and learned Python, HTML, CSS, and a little bit of Java on my own before high school ended. I am looking to learn and master a lot more languages in the future. The source code for some of my projects can be found on my GitHub.
